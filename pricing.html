Common Pricing Questions

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes. All our paid plans are month-to-month by default. You can cancel your subscription from your account settings at any time with no hidden fees.

What happens to my slides if I downgrade?

Your existing decks remain in your account. However, you will lose access to premium features like non-watermarked exports and custom brand fonts until you upgrade again.

Do you offer team or enterprise discounts?

We do! For teams of 5 or more, please contact our sales department for custom pricing and dedicated brand management features.

Is my data secure in Free AI Slides?

We take data privacy seriously. Your inputs are used only to generate your slides and are not used to train global AI models without your explicit consent.
